About Us

Where Data Meets Insight

"Ceteris Paribus" – Latin for "all else being equal" – is the premier Data Science Club at Maqsut Narikbayev University. We are dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of data science and its applications across various disciplines.

Our mission is to foster a community of data enthusiasts who collaborate, learn, and innovate together. We believe in the power of data to transform how we understand the world and make decisions.

From machine learning to statistical analysis, from data visualization to predictive modeling, we cover the entire spectrum of data science to empower students with the skills needed in today's data-driven world.
